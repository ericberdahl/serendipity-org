Parade - Open

Good morning ladies and gentlemen. Welcome to the Parade Competition for the 48th Annual Foothill High School Band Review. We are joined today by more than thirty wonderful bands to entertain and delight us. Foothill High School wishes good luck and good music to all the bands performing today. We hope you enjoy the competition.

Parade - Presentation of the Colors

As we start our parade, please rise, remove your caps, and give your attention and respect to our colors, presented by the color guard from Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 6298.

Parade - Grand Marshal

And now… please welcome our Grand Marshal, long time Pleasanton resident, KTVU Metereologist, and 7-time Emmy Award Winner, Roberta Gonzales.

Parade - Recurring Announcements

  • As a courtesy to the performers, spectators please step back fully onto the curb to allow the band to use the entire street.
  • As our parade continues, bands will get progressively larger.
