Parade - Open

Good morning ladies and gentlemen. Welcome to the Parade Competition for the 45th Annual Foothill High School Band Review. We are joined today by nearly two score wonderful bands to entertain and delight us. On behalf of Foothill High School, we wish good luck to all the bands performing today and hope you enjoy the competition.

Parade - Grand Marshal

Leading off today’s parade, please welcome our Grand Marshal, Pleasanton Mayor, Jerry Thorne.

Parade - Presentation of the Colors

At this time, please rise and give your attention and respect to the Presentation of the Colors by the color guard from Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 6298.
