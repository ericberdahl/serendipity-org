Parade - Open

Good morning ladies and gentlemen. Welcome to the Parade Competition for the 44th Annual Foothill High School Band Review. We are joined today by more than thirty wonderful bands to entertain and delight us. On behalf of Foothill High School, we wish good luck to all the bands performing today and hope you enjoy the competition.

Parade - Grand Marshal

Leading off today’s parade, please welcome our Grand Marshalls, Foothill High School Principal Sebastain Bull, and Foothill High School Admnistrative Secretary Ruth Highstreet.

Parade - Presentation of the Colors

At this time, please rise and give respect to the colors, presented by by the color guard from Veterans of Foreign Wars Post 6298.
